on silence (and why i prefer it)

June 20, 2025

There was a time I filled every quiet moment — podcasts while walking, playlists while cleaning, messages and updates always whispering (or shouting) in the background. But lately, I’ve come to prefer the hush.

Silence isn’t empty. It’s full — of presence, breath, memory. It’s where the noise settles and the real things surface.

When I’m quiet, I notice more. I feel more. I remember who I am when no one’s watching or liking or refreshing.

This isn’t a manifesto. Just a margin note from a slower life. I don’t know what comes next — only that it will arrive more gently if I’m listening for it.
