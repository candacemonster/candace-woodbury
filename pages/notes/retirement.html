retiring from the internet (again)

June 22, 2025

I’ve left the internet before — sometimes with a dramatic goodbye, sometimes like slipping out of a party no one noticed I came to. And still, I return. A post here. A project there. Just a toe back in the stream.

This time feels different. Not dramatic. Not burned out. Just... done. The noise is too much. The performance too constant. The self I bring here too far from the one I actually know.

Maybe this is temporary. Maybe not. Either way, I’m letting go again — not in protest, but in offering. A little silence returned to the world.

If I reappear, I hope it’s quieter. If I don’t, I hope it’s because the silence is finally enough.
