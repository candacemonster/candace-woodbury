## privacy

this is a personal site and notebook, not a data-harvesting operation, but some information still moves around.

- if you email me, i see your email address and whatever you chose to send. i use it to reply and keep our conversation.  
- server logs and basic analytics may collect anonymous information like pages viewed, timestamps, and general location (country/region). this helps me understand what’s being read, not who you are.  
- if i ever add a newsletter or contact form, i’ll collect whatever you enter there (like your email) for the obvious purpose of sending things you explicitly signed up for.

i don't sell or rent your personal information. if a third‑party tool is used (for example: analytics or embedded content), it may place its own cookies or track usage according to its own policies.

if you want to know what information i have about you (which is usually “emails you sent me”), you can email me at `hello@candacewoodbury.com`.

## affiliate links & recommendations

some pages and posts include links to products, services, or apps. some of those links may be affiliate links.

what that means in practice:

- if you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, i may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.  
- i only link to things i actually use, have used, or genuinely believe are useful. i don’t accept payment in exchange for positive reviews.  
- affiliate links are identified in context (on the gear page and in posts) and may include a short note or disclosure at the top of the post.

if this ever changes—sponsored posts, gifted gear, etc.—it will be clearly labeled so you can decide how much weight to give it.
