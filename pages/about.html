I’ve been online long enough to know better. I used to post everywhere, convinced that someone might care. They didn’t. Now I don’t.

This site exists because I still believe in websites — not feeds, not apps, not the latest thing pretending not to be Twitter. Just a plain place to put things.

What’s here? Whatever survives. The projects I haven’t abandoned. A few notes that made it past the draft folder. Nothing optimized, monetized, or maintained with any regularity.

...and if you're still curious for some reason:

  • I’m Candace. I write code, mostly poorly.
  • I drink too much coffee and ride my bike in the desert.
  • I prefer silence, solitude, and slow things.
  • I’m allergic to hustle, urgency, and anything requiring a sign-up.

No newsletter. No promises. No urgency. You're welcome.
